<Info>
This page covers your personal notification preferences. If you're an admin looking to configure organization-wide settings, see [Tenant notification settings](/product/admin/notifications-tenant-settings).
</Info>

Customize your notification preferences in **Settings** > **Notifications**, or by clicking your profile icon and selecting **Notification preferences**.

Your notification settings are based on defaults configured by your organization's admins. Depending on your organization's settings, you may be able to customize some or all of your notification preferences.

## Locked and unlocked settings

- **Unlocked settings**: You can customize these preferences to match your needs.
- **Locked settings** (shown with a lock icon): These are set by your admin and cannot be changed.

If a setting you need is locked, contact your admin to request a change.

## Email notifications

### Email address

Notifications are sent to the email address associated with your C1 account. To update your email address, contact your admin.

### Digest frequency

Choose how often you receive digest emails:

- **Daily**: Delivered on weekdays at 9 AM Pacific time.
- **Weekly**: Delivered on Tuesdays at 10 AM Pacific time.
- **Never**: Opt out of digest emails.

Digest emails are only sent if you have at least one pending task or expiring access item.

To change your digest frequency:

<Steps>
<Step>
Navigate to **Settings** > **Notifications**.
</Step>
<Step>
Under **Email preferences**, select **Digest frequency**.
</Step>
<Step>
Choose **Daily**, **Weekly**, or **Never**.
</Step>
</Steps>

If this setting is locked, a lock icon will appear and the option cannot be changed.

### Notification types

Control which types of notifications you receive via email.

**Task summaries**

- Open tasks requiring your action.
- Pending access requests awaiting your approval.
- Access reviews assigned to you.
- Escalated requests.

**Access expiration alerts**

Notifications sent before your access to an application or resource expires.

To configure your notification types:

<Steps>
<Step>
Navigate to **Settings** > **Notifications**.
</Step>
<Step>
Under **Email preferences**, toggle each notification type on or off.
</Step>
</Steps>

If a notification type is locked, a lock icon will appear and the option cannot be changed.

## Notification eligibility

**Digest emails**: Only sent if you have at least one pending task or expiring access item. If you have nothing requiring attention, no digest is sent.

**Access expiration alerts**: Only sent if you have access that is about to expire, according to your organization's expiration alert schedule.

## Best practices

- **Choose an appropriate frequency**: Select daily digests if you handle time-sensitive approvals. Weekly digests work well for periodic access reviews.
- **Enable multiple channels**: Enable Slack or Teams for real-time notifications while keeping email enabled for a permanent record.
- **Review periodically**: Check your notification preferences quarterly to ensure they match your current role and responsibilities.
- **Update when roles change**: If your responsibilities change, update your notification preferences to reflect your new priorities.
