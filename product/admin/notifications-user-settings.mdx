<Info>
This page covers your personal notification preferences. If you're an admin looking to configure organization-wide settings, see [Tenant notification settings](/product/admin/notifications-tenant-settings).
</Info>

## Update your notification preferences

To view your current notification settings and make updates:

<Steps>
<Step>
In C1, click your profile icon at the bottom left of the screen and select **Notification settings**.
</Step>
<Step>
In the **Notifications** area of the page, click **Edit**.
</Step>
<Step>
Adjust your notification preferences and delivery methods, as needed. If a setting is locked by your organizations' C1 admins and cannot be changed, the checkbox will be disabled.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Save** to apply your changes.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Locked and unlocked notification settings

Your available personal notification settings are based on defaults configured by your organization's admins. Depending on your organization's settings, you may be able to customize some or all of your notification preferences.

- **Unlocked settings**: You can customize these preferences to match your needs.
- **Locked settings** (disabled checkbox): These are set by your admin and cannot be changed.

If a setting you need to adjust is locked, contact your C1 admin to request a change.

## Update your C1 digest delivery preferences

Sign up to receive digest notifications on a daily or weekly cadence. Digests contain a summary of the C1 tasks requiring your attention. 

Digest notifications are only sent if you have at least one pending task or expiring access item. If you have nothing requiring attention, no digest is sent.

<Steps>
<Step>
In C1, click your profile icon at the bottom left of the screen and select **Notification settings**.
</Step>
<Step>
In the **C1 digest** area of the page, click **Edit**.
</Step>
<Step>
If necessary, click the toggle to enable **Send C1 digest**.
</Step>
<Step>
Choose how often you'll receive digests:

- **Daily**: Delivered on weekdays at 9 AM Pacific time.
- **Weekly**: Delivered on the weekday of your choice at 10 AM Pacific time.
</Step>
<Step>
Click to select how you'll receive digests: via email, Slack, or Microsoft Teams.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Save** to apply your changes.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Make your notifications work for you

Here are some tips to help you get the most value out of your C1 notifications:

- **Choose an appropriate frequency**: Select daily digests if you handle time-sensitive approvals. Weekly digests work well for periodic access reviews.
- **Enable multiple channels**: Enable Slack or Teams for real-time notifications while keeping email enabled for a permanent record.
- **Review periodically**: Check your notification preferences quarterly to ensure they match your current role and responsibilities.
- **Update when roles change**: If your responsibilities change, update your notification preferences to reflect your new priorities.
