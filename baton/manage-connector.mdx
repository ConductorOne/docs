**Important**

This task requires the **Super Administrator** or **Application Administrator** role in ConductorOne.
</Warning>

## Rename a connector

You can rename a connector to make it easier to identify in your ConductorOne instance.

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Navigate to the **Apps** page and select an application.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    On the application's details page, locate the connector name at the top of the page.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click the pencil icon next to the connector name.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Enter the new name for the connector.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click outside the field or press Enter to save your changes.
    </Step>
</Steps>

**That's it!** The connector has been renamed.

## Manage connector owners

Connector owners are responsible for managing and maintaining the connector. You can add, change, or remove connector owners as needed.

### Add a connector owner

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Navigate to the **Apps** page and select an application.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    On the application's details page, click **…** (more actions) and select **Configure connector**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    In the **Owners** section, click **Add owner**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Select a user from the list of ConductorOne users.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click **Save**.
    </Step>
</Steps>

The new owner will receive an email notification about their new responsibility.

### Remove a connector owner

<Steps>
    <Step>
    Navigate to the **Apps** page and select an application.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    On the application's details page, click **…** (more actions) and select **Configure connector**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    In the **Owners** section, locate the owner you want to remove.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click the **X** or remove icon next to the owner's name.
    </Step>
    <Step>
    Click **Save**.
    </Step>
</Steps>

**That's it!** The connector owner has been removed.
