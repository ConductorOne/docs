## Configure the Google Ads connector

<Warning>
**To complete this task, you'll need:**

- The **Connector Administrator** or **Super Administrator** role in C1
- A Google Ads account with a developer token
- A service account credentials JSON file
</Warning>

## Required credentials

- `credentials-json-file-path` — path to your Google service account credentials JSON file
- `developer-token` — your Google Ads developer token
- `customer-id` — your Google Ads customer ID
